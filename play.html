🎮 Play now

Two ways to play

Boot up the browser colony sim for a solo strategy game, or gather a crew and run a collaborative D&D-style adventure with an AI Space Wizard as your guide.

Option 1 · Solo / screen

🚀 Lunar & Martian Frontier — the colony sim

A standalone sandbox that blends SimCity, Catan, and Civilization. Runs entirely in your browser — no sign-up, no API keys, no downloads. Best on a desktop with a mouse.

↗ Open the game full-screen

🏙️

Grow a colony

Train rover crews and shuttle colonists from Earth. Nothing builds itself — rovers physically drive out and construct every structure.

🎲

Catan-style yields

Every hex carries a 2–12 number token. Two dice roll each sol; matching tiles surge and yield double. A 7 triggers a colony event.

🫁

Life-support loop

Colonists burn food, water, and oxygen every sol. Extract ice, make air, grow food, keep the power grid ahead of demand — or everything throttles.

🛤️

Maglev network

Structures only work next to the Hub or a connected track. Cut the line and everything downstream goes offline.

⚔️

Rival AI colonies

Up to three AI factions land beside you — traders, racers, and agrarians — each with its own strategy and colours.

🤖

Autoplay & stats

Hit AUTOPLAY to watch an AI director build the base, or open the dashboard to graph every colony's growth over time.

Option 2 · Team / imagination

🧙 The D&D-style role-play adventure

Gather a crew (in person or online), give everyone a role, and let an AI "Space Wizard" act as your Dungeon Master. You can play it purely in the imagination, or over a 2D / 3D board.

Each player takes a role — Space Wizard (Dungeon Master), Graphic Designer, Medical Lead, Coder, Librarian, Military Lead, Farmer & Scientist — and uses AI tools to contribute to each turn. Roll 2d6, face a challenge, and decide together how your mission to Mars unfolds.

  • Design your own character and their strengths
  • Face staged challenges: launch, cryosleep, landing, farming, radiation storms
  • The Space Wizard narrates outcomes and sets up the next turn
Get the AI Space-Wizard guide →
New to role-play? No problem. The AI role-play guide gives you copy-paste prompts that turn any modern chatbot (ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, Claude) into your Dungeon Master — no prior D&D experience needed.

Prefer a physical board?

3D-print the Martian terrain pieces, paint them, and play around a real table.
