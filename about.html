📖 About

The story behind Settlers of Mars

An open, remixable educational project that teaches space biology, biotechnology, and aerospace engineering through play.

What it is

Settlers of Mars is a choose-your-own-adventure that merges the strategic resource-management of Settlers of Catan, the collaborative storytelling of Dungeons & Dragons, and the survival-sim loop of SimCity / Civilization. It exists in three forms that share one world:

  • A browser colony sim — the Lunar & Martian Frontier — playable instantly online.
  • An AI-guided tabletop role-play — any chatbot becomes your "Space Wizard" Dungeon Master.
  • A 3D-printable physical board — download, print, paint, and play.

The point is to fill the gap left by formal science education: the informal, hands-on, team-based skills that make real space research work. Every mechanic maps to a genuine challenge of living on another world.

Origin

The game began as a student prototype in the Insight Wisconsin inventors' club, built by a team that wanted to know whether a board game could teach the science of planetary settlement. It has since grown 3D-printable pieces, AI-generated boards, five levels of AI Dungeon-Master prompts, and a full browser sim.

Credits

Created by Dr Richard Barker on behalf of The Collaborative Science Environment (cosecloud.com), building on the original Insight Wisconsin inventors'-club prototype. The browser colony sim (Lunar & Martian Frontier) is a companion project. Inspired by Klaus Teuber's Settlers of Catan and by NASA's open-science community.
